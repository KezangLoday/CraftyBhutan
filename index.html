WELCOME TO CRAFTY BHUTAN

The need of spreading the Bhutanese handicraft has been very confined which is only within Bhutan over the time there had rapid revolution in virtual where Bhutan has seen some positive effect with the aid of e-commerce.

A Shop for Authentic Handmade Bhutanese Products

Born from our desire to share our culture, we created a platform that connect passionate producers with distinctive buyers. With our shop, we seek to empower Bhutanese craft-makers by extending their products reach far beyond our borders.

Behind each of our products is an actual person, a family, a community who crafted with knowledge passed down generation to generation and who truly benefit from each and every sale they make. By buying from our shop, you are taking a stand with your money to directly provide meaningful income to our artisans.

A Word About Us

The idea has been proposed to set up on the international marketing of Bhutanese handicraft products.

The business basically focuses on purchasing of Bhutanese made handicrafts of various raw materials that are shaped into fine goods.
